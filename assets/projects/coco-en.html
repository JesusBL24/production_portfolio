El Coco

El Coco is an action rogue-lite where players take on the role of Ky, a small lost soul trapped within The Uncertain—the realm of dreams. Their mission is to descend through The Hole into its deepest layers, facing nightmares and overcoming different types of challenges, enemies and bosses.

Game definition, target audience and pipeline

Our first steps were to define as a team what we were building and who was the target audience of this game. For this, we used Game Pillars and Goals/Values (what are our objectives and what we optimize for on achieving those) to define the experience and align the team.

Then, we established what would be our iterative pipeline for the full preproduction and production based on problem statement and testing, maximizing our iteration speed while improving the game upong gathered feedback.
